9. Protection of Minors

 

We attach great importance to the protection of personal information of minors and continuously explore new approaches to safeguarding their personal information. Our products refuse service to underage users, so please ensure that you are at least 18 years old when using our product and services. We will not be held responsible for any personal account losses resulting from non-compliance with this restriction.

 

In accordance with relevant regulations on preventing addiction to online games, we will implement an anti-addiction system to protect the legitimate rights and interests of minors. We will determine whether an account belongs to a minor based on the identification results of real-name authentication and decide whether to include that account in the anti-addiction system. Additionally, we will collect information such as your login time and game duration, and guide minors to play games rationally through automatic interventions and limitations on game time. For minors within our system, we will enable features like mandatory game exit. When suspected underage individuals make purchases, we will make every effort to contact their parents or legal guardians for reminders, confirmation, and handling, thus promoting healthy internet usage among minors.

 

Furthermore, in order to better protect the physical and mental health of minors and facilitate their healthy internet access, we may adopt stricter anti-addiction measures based on relevant policies concerning internet addiction. Additionally, we will continue researching and testing various technologies for protecting minors. For example, in order to further improve the accuracy of real-name authentication, and to prevent minors from impersonating others, we may implement facial recognition verification in certain games or for specific users, depending on the circumstances (such as during game login or when making recharge, purchase, or transaction activities within the game).

 

Moreover, we may collect gameplay data generated during your gaming process (such as pressure and radius of clicks formed when operating the game, relative positions of clicks, acceleration direction, gravity direction, and other data), voice data on your terminal device, and voice data generated when interacting with other players through online voice chat in the game. These data will be used to make preliminary judgments about whether the user is a minor (regarding voice data, the system will only use a random portion of your online voice data to determine if it belongs to a child or an adult). If there is a preliminary determination that you are a minor, we may activate facial recognition verification as mentioned earlier.

 

If you are a parent or legal guardian of a minor, please ensure that the minor only uses our services or provides their personal information with your authorized consent. If you have any questions regarding the personal information of a minor under your care, please contact us using the contact information provided in Clause 10 of this agreement.
